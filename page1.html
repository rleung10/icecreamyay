ICE CREAM!



Ice cream flavors!

What are some of the most popular ice cream flavors?

There are countless ice cream flavors, but the most common flavors are vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry. On top of the actual ice cream flavor, there are countless toppings and combinations to choose from. My personal favorite is a vanilla-caramel swirl with chocolate chips.

Classic Flavors

Chocolate

Chocolate is one of the most iconic ice cream flavors. Made with cocoa or chocolate flavoring, the chocolate flavor sure tastes amazing.

Check out a recipe here: Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe; just add cocoa for the chocolate flavor

Strawberry

Strawberry ice cream is made with strawberry flavorings, or even better, fresh strawberries. Strawberries have a sweet but a vague "tart" taste, which makes the resulting ice cream extremely yummy.

See this recipe: Strawberry Ice Cream Recipe

Vanilla

Vanilla ice cream is made from vanilla extract, or vanilla bean. Vanilla is considered the original ice cream flavor, and one of, if not the most popular ice cream flavors IN EXISTENCE.

See a Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe: Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe, add vanilla bean or extract

Rocky Road

Rocky Road is a type of ice cream that usually consists of chocolate ice cream with nuts and marshmallows(that give it the "Rocky" name).
See a Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe: Ice Cream Recipe (add chocolate)





Tropical Flavors

There are SO many tropical flavors of ice cream. These can include varieties of tropical fruit. Two flavors are mango ice cream; made with mangoes(yum), or Pina Colada; the amazing Coconut and Pineapple duo.

Check out this list of tropical flavored ice cream: Amazing Tropical Ice Cream flavors
