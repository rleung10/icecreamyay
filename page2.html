ICE CREAM!



What are some other desserts like ice cream?

Gelato

Gelato is a popular frozen dessert of Italian origin. Gelato is usually fruit flavored, and is generally made with a base of 3.25% milk and sugar. It it also lower in fat than say ice cream.

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gelato





Sorbet

Sorbet is a type of frozen dessert made ENTIRELY from fruit or juice. Sorbet usually contains no milk, rather than sherbet; which is a mix of both juice/water and milk.

See this source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sorbet





Shaved Ice and Popsicles

Popsicles are flavored water/mixture frozen into a bar. A similar dessert, shaved ice is made with syrup(similar to a snow cone, except the ice is shaved not crushed).
Shaved ice and popsicles usually have NO milk content, and are artificially flavored/flavored from fruit.
